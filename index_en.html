Our Story

SuccessWay Mali was founded in July 2018 with the mission of creating a bridge between Malian students and international educational opportunities.

2018

Founded in Bamako

Founded in Bamako, Mali, with the vision of helping students access quality education abroad.

2020

Regional Expansion

Extended operations to Ouagadougou, Niamey, Conakry, and Abidjan to serve more students.

2022

Strategic Partnerships

Established strategic partnerships with universities in Canada, Turkey, and beyond.

Today

Continuing Our Mission

Continuing our mission with a growing team and hundreds of successful student placements.

Mission and Vision

Our commitment to educational excellence

Our Mission

Equip students with the knowledge, resources, and support necessary to pursue higher education abroad, fostering a new generation of globally-minded professionals.

Our Vision

A world where every student, regardless of their background, has access to quality international education through personalized guidance and a smooth transition to studying abroad.
