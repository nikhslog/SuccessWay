Important Information

  • SuccessWay can facilitate admission to partner colleges in Montreal and Gatineau, but not directly to universities
  • New high school graduates will find it difficult to get university admission directly and may need to go through college first
  • Bachelor's degree holders may have difficulty transferring credits to universities
  • Students can work part-time but cannot fully support themselves
  • Visa processing time varies from 1-12 months and is determined by immigration officials

Application Process

  1. First agency fee payment (600,000 FCFA) + Admission fees
    Submit your documents and make the first payment to begin the process.
  2. Obtaining admission letter
    Once your application is processed, you'll receive an official letter of admission.
  3. Visa application (CAQ and Study Permit)
    Apply for the Quebec Acceptance Certificate and Study Permit.
  4. Biometric appointment and waiting period
    Complete biometric data collection and wait for processing.
  5. Medical examination and passport submission
    After file approval, undergo medical examination and submit your passport.
  6. Visa issuance and remaining agency fee payment
    Once your visa is approved, pay the remaining agency fee.
  7. Purchase flight ticket and first tuition payment
    Prepare for departure by purchasing your flight and paying your first tuition installment.

  • Minimum age requirement: 16 years at enrollment

  • Must present a bank statement (from a close parent) proving financial capacity of at least 20 million FCFA
